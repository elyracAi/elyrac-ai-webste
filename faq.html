Process & Timeline

Most projects take 4–12 weeks depending on complexity. Simple automations can be live in 3–4 weeks. Complex multi-system AI platforms may take 3–4 months. We agree on a clear timeline in the scoping phase before any work begins.

We handle all the technical work, but we need your input to do it right. Expect 2–3 hours per week for feedback, domain knowledge sharing, and review sessions. We work iteratively, so your team sees progress weekly — not just at the end.
