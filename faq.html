About elyrac AI

Off-the-shelf AI tools are built for everyone — which means they're optimized for no one specific. We build AI systems engineered specifically for your business: your data, your workflows, your edge cases, your goals. The difference is like buying a suit versus having one tailored to fit you exactly. Ours fits because it was designed to.

We work with businesses of all sizes — from founder-led companies with 5 people to larger organizations with hundreds of staff. What they have in common: they have a real operational problem, they've outgrown generic tools, and they want AI that actually changes a business metric. If that sounds like you, we should talk.

We're industry-agnostic at the core. Our AI engineering methodology adapts to any domain. We've built systems for legal, healthcare, retail, logistics, fintech, education, hospitality, real estate, and government. If your business generates data and has repeatable processes, there's an AI opportunity — and we'll help you find and capture it.

Working With Us

Absolutely. We work with non-technical founders and business owners all the time. We own the entire build — from architecture through deployment — so you never need to manage developers, coordinate between vendors, or translate between technical and business teams. You describe the business problem. We solve it.

It starts with a free 30-minute strategy call where we learn about your business and identify the highest-impact AI opportunity. From there, we move into a scoping phase where we define the system architecture and scope. Once aligned, we begin building — and most projects go from kickoff to deployed system in 4–10 weeks depending on complexity.

Integration is a core part of every project. We design AI systems to work alongside your existing stack — CRMs, ERPs, databases, APIs, and custom-built tools. We use industry-standard integration patterns and webhooks so your new AI layer fits into what you already have rather than replacing it.

Technical & Security

Data privacy and security are first-class concerns in everything we build. We follow industry best practices: encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, data minimization, and full audit logging. We design every system to comply with relevant regulations, and we never use your data to train third-party models without explicit permission.
