Inventory AI System
01
AI Automation · Supply Chain

Inventory AI System

The Problem

A regional distribution company was managing over 12,000 SKUs manually — relying on spreadsheets and gut instinct to decide what to order and when. The result was constant overstock on slow-moving items and frustrating stockouts on fast movers, costing them tens of thousands monthly in waste and lost sales.

What We Built

A demand forecasting and inventory optimization system that ingests historical sales data, seasonal patterns, and supplier lead times to predict required stock levels for each SKU up to 8 weeks ahead. The system auto-triggers reorder requests and flags anomalies in real time.

The Result

Waste reduced by 78% in the first 90 days. Stockout events dropped by 64%. The operations team now spends their time on exceptions rather than manual counting — and they shipped the system in under 2 months.

Legal Document Assistant

The Problem

A legal services firm was receiving over 200 documents per day — NDAs, contracts, compliance reports, and regulatory filings. Junior associates were spending 6–8 hours daily on first-pass review before any substantive legal work could begin. The bottleneck was eating into capacity and increasing client wait times.

What We Built

An AI document assistant that reads any incoming legal document, extracts key clauses and obligations, flags risk sections based on customizable risk criteria, cross-references against a proprietary precedent library, and produces a structured summary in under 60 seconds. Fully integrated into their existing document management workflow.

The Result

First-pass review time dropped by 85%. Associates can now process the full daily document load in under an hour, spending the remaining time on high-value analysis. The system processes 200+ documents per day without performance degradation.

03
Conversational AI · E-commerce

Customer Support AI Agent

The Problem

A fast-growing e-commerce brand was receiving 400–600 support tickets daily across email, website chat, and WhatsApp. Their 5-person support team was underwater, response times had ballooned to 14 hours, and customer satisfaction scores were dropping. Hiring more agents wasn't a sustainable solution.

What We Built

A multi-channel AI agent with full product catalog access, order tracking integration, return policy knowledge, and contextual memory across a conversation. The agent handles entire support interactions end-to-end, escalates to humans only when genuinely needed, and logs everything structured for analysis. Deployed across WhatsApp, email, and website chat simultaneously.

The Result

70% of all tickets are now resolved autonomously without any human involvement. Average response time dropped from 14 hours to under 2 minutes. Customer satisfaction scores recovered to 98%. The support team now handles only complex escalations — which freed them to work on retention instead of firefighting.
