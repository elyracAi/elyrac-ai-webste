About elyrac AI

Who We Are

elyrac AI is a custom artificial intelligence engineering company headquartered in Kampala, Uganda. We design, develop, and deploy fully bespoke AI systems — built specifically around the operational realities and strategic ambitions of each client we work with.

Unlike AI vendors selling subscriptions to generic tools, we engineer from the ground up. Every system we build is purpose-built: the architecture, the data pipeline, the model integrations, the user interface, the security layer — designed to solve your exact problem, not a hypothetical average one.

Our motto is Grace in Design — we believe that the best AI systems are not just technically excellent but thoughtfully crafted, intuitive to use, and built to last. We have delivered over 30 AI projects across healthcare, fintech, HR tech, NGOs, and enterprise operations.

We work with forward-thinking businesses, growing enterprises, and institutions that understand that AI is not a feature — it is infrastructure. And infrastructure deserves to be engineered properly.

Our Work — Case Studies

Selected Projects

Real AI systems built for real businesses. Every result below is measured and verified.

01 — Intelligent Email Agent · ioTec Ltd
ENTERPRISE AI
Challenge
The team was spending 4+ hours daily on email triage and drafting responses to routine enquiries. The inbox had become a bottleneck, slowing down sales and support cycles.
Solution
Custom AI email agent that reads, categorises, drafts responses, and escalates complex cases to the right team member — fully integrated with their existing inbox.
Result
80% of emails handled autonomously. 3+ hours reclaimed daily. Response times dropped from hours to minutes.
02 — AttendTrack · Workforce Management System
HR TECH
Challenge
Manual attendance tracking and zero visibility into field worker locations. HR teams relied on spreadsheets and guesswork, causing payroll errors.
Solution
Full workforce management platform with AI-powered attendance, real-time GPS geofencing, automated payroll (NSSF/PAYE), and an AI Copilot powered by Claude Sonnet.
Result
100% attendance accuracy. 3 hours saved daily on HR admin. Payroll errors eliminated completely.
03 — Code Clinic · Healthcare Practice Management
HEALTHTECH
Challenge
Fragmented tools with no unified system. Patient records, appointments, billing, and staff management were all handled separately with no real-time visibility.
Solution
Comprehensive clinic management platform covering patient records, live appointment scheduling, invoicing, payroll, staff management, and a full analytics dashboard.
Result
790 active patients managed seamlessly. Revenue tracked in real-time (UGX 31.2M monthly). Admin workload reduced by 60%.
Our Work — Case Studies (continued)
04 — WinoPay · Full-Stack Fintech Platform
FINTECH
Challenge
Building a production-grade financial platform from scratch demanding the highest standards of security, data integrity, and regulatory compliance for real-money transactions.
Solution
Full system architecture end-to-end — backend API design, transaction processing, complete security layer with audit logging, RBAC, and encryption. Integrates flights, hotels, bill pay, insurance, and retail.
Result
Fully operational fintech platform serving live users. Zero critical transaction failures since launch. Meets regulated financial institution compliance standards.
05 — Trivox AI · Intelligent Dental Receptionist
CONVERSATIONAL AI
Challenge
Dental clinics losing patients due to missed calls, slow booking responses, and overwhelmed front desk staff. After-hours enquiries went completely unanswered.
Solution
Fully conversational AI receptionist trained on dental clinic workflows — handles appointment booking, patient enquiries, follow-up reminders, and proactive outreach 24/7.
Result
+40% more bookings captured. 24/7 availability with zero missed outreaches. Front desk workload cut in half.
06 — AI/ML Fraud Detection · Financial Services
FINTECH / REGTECH
Challenge
Legacy rule-based systems generating too many false positives while missing sophisticated fraud patterns — a compliance and financial liability risk at transaction scale.
Solution
Purpose-built ML fraud detection pipeline — data ingestion, feature engineering, model training, real-time inference, compliance alerts with full explanations, and complete audit trail.
Result
Real-time fraud detection at transaction scale. Full regulatory audit trail. Significant reduction in false positives. Model continuously improves with new data.
