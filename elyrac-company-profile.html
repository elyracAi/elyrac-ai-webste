01 · About Us
Who We Are

ABOUT US

elyrac AI is a custom artificial intelligence engineering company headquartered in Kampala, Uganda. We design, develop, and deploy fully bespoke AI systems — built specifically around the operational realities and strategic ambitions of each client we work with.

Unlike vendors selling generic AI subscriptions, we engineer from the ground up. Every system is purpose-built: the architecture, data pipeline, model integrations, user interface, and security layer — designed to solve your exact problem.

Our motto is Grace in Design — the best AI systems are not just technically excellent, but thoughtfully crafted, intuitive to use, and built to last. We have delivered over 30 AI projects across healthcare, fintech, HR tech, NGOs, and enterprise operations.

01 — Intelligent Email Agent · ioTec Ltd
ENTERPRISE AI
Challenge
4+ hours daily lost to email triage, categorisation, and drafting responses. The inbox had become a business bottleneck slowing down sales and support cycles.
Solution
Custom AI email agent that reads, categorises, drafts responses, and escalates complex cases to the right team member — fully integrated into their existing inbox workflow.
Result
80% of emails handled autonomously. 3+ hours reclaimed daily. Response times dropped from hours to minutes.
02 — AttendTrack · Workforce Management System
HR TECH
Challenge
Manual attendance tracking and zero field visibility. HR teams used spreadsheets and guesswork — causing payroll errors and no accountability for field teams.
Solution
Full workforce platform with AI-powered attendance, real-time GPS geofencing, automated payroll (NSSF/PAYE), and an AI Copilot powered by Claude Sonnet for natural language HR queries.
Result
100% attendance accuracy. 3 hours saved daily on HR admin. Payroll errors eliminated completely.
03 — Code Clinic · Healthcare Practice Management
HEALTHTECH
Challenge
Fragmented tools with no unified system. Patient records, appointments, billing, and staff management were all handled separately with no real-time visibility.
Solution
Comprehensive clinic platform covering patient records, live appointment scheduling, invoicing, payroll, staff management, and a full analytics dashboard — one unified system.
Result
790 active patients managed. Revenue tracked in real-time (UGX 31.2M monthly). Admin workload reduced by 60%.

04 — WinoPay · Full-Stack Fintech Platform
FINTECH
Challenge
Building a production-grade financial platform from scratch — the highest standards of security, data integrity, and regulatory compliance for real-money transactions across multiple services.
Solution
Full system architecture — backend API, database structure, transaction processing, complete security layer with audit logging, RBAC, and encryption. Integrates flights, hotels, bill pay, insurance, and retail.
Result
Zero critical transaction failures since launch. Meets regulated financial institution compliance standards.
05 — Trivox AI · Intelligent Dental Receptionist
CONVERSATIONAL AI
Challenge
Dental clinics losing patients due to missed calls, slow booking responses, and overwhelmed front desk staff. After-hours enquiries went completely unanswered.
Solution
Fully conversational AI receptionist trained on dental clinic workflows — handles appointment booking, patient enquiries, follow-up reminders, and proactive outreach 24/7.
Result
+40% more bookings captured. 24/7 availability. Front desk workload cut in half.
06 — AI/ML Fraud Detection · Financial Services
FINTECH / REGTECH
Challenge
Legacy rule-based systems generating too many false positives while missing sophisticated fraud patterns — a compliance and financial liability risk at transaction scale.
Solution
Purpose-built ML fraud detection pipeline — data ingestion, feature engineering, model training, real-time inference, compliance alerts with full explanations, and complete regulatory audit trail.
Result
Real-time detection at transaction scale. Full regulatory audit trail. Significant reduction in false positives.
